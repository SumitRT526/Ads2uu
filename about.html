Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum budget required?

We recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹15,000 - ₹20,000 per month to see significant results, separate from our service fee.

How long does it take to see results?

Typically, we see initial traction within the first 7-14 days. Full optimization and stable results usually occur within the first month.

Do you work with local businesses?

Yes! We specialize in helping local businesses like gyms, salons, clinics, and real estate agents generate high-quality leads.

What is included in your service fee?

Our fee covers strategy, campaign setup, ad creative production (including UGC), ongoing management, optimization, and reporting.

Is ad spend included in your pricing?

No, ad spend is paid directly to the advertising platform (Meta, Google) and is billed separately.

Can you build a website for me?

Absolutely. We design and develop high-performance websites optimized for conversions and SEO.

How do we communicate?

We use WhatsApp for quick updates and schedule regular Zoom/Google Meet calls for strategy reviews.
